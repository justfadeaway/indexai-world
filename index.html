Founders

Built by the people who build frontier models

Our founding team comes from the labs and companies defining this era of AI — Google DeepMind, OpenAI, Anthropic, Stanford and TikTok.

The founding team includes core contributors to the Gemini model ecosystem at Google DeepMind, with deep expertise in Large Language Models — particularly data strategy and Reinforcement Learning (RL) post-training — alongside founders with research and operating backgrounds spanning OpenAI, Anthropic, Stanford and TikTok.

Together, the team has architected end-to-end, autonomous data synthesis pipelines and rigorous quality-assurance frameworks to consistently supply models with high-fidelity training data, and driven critical advancements in value functions and step-level reward mechanisms to resolve complex credit-assignment challenges in long-horizon reasoning tasks.

Beyond these core areas, their work extends to spearheading multi-step planning engines for multimodal agentic systems.
